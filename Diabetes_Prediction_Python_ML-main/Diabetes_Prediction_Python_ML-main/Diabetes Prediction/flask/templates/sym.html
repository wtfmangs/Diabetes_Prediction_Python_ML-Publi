Diabetes Symptoms


Explore common diabetes symptoms: frequent thirst, increased urination, and unexplained weight loss. Early recognition is key to effective management. Let's navigate through these signs together for better understanding and proactive health care.

Possible Symptoms

  • Frequent thirst
  • Increased urination
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow healing wounds
  • Tingling or numbness in hands or feet
  • Recurrent infections
  • Dry, itchy skin
  • Increased hunger, especially after eating
